Rules

Quick summary

On your turn, either (1) pass by paying a chip into the center or (2) take the card in the center along with any chips in the center. If you take the center card, flip over the next card in the deck and either pass by paying a chip or take the card. Continue taking cards until you pass, at which point play continues to the next player. The game ends when all cards have been taken. Your score is the sum of the lowest cards in every consecutive sequence of cards you have minus the number of chips you have. The player with the lowest score wins.

Setup

The deck has 33 numbered cards, from 3 to 35, one card for each number.

Shuffle the deck and remove 9 random cards from the game without looking at them.

Deal 11 chips to each player.

Choose the starting player randomly.

Gameplay

The starting player turns over the top card of the deck and places it face up in the common area the table.

On your turn, you must do either PASS or TAKE CARD.

PASS: Place one of your chips on the face-up card.

TAKE CARD: Take the face-up card in the common area of the table and place it face up in front of you. When you take a card, you also collect all the chips on the card. You then turn over the next card from the deck and place it face up in the common area. You must now either PASS or TAKE CARD again.

If you do not have any chips, you cannot pass and must instead take the face-up card.

Once you have taken the PASS action, play continues to the next player.

Play continues until all cards in the deck have been taken. When the last card is taken, the game ends immediately.

Scoring

Each card you have face up in front of you is worth points according to its number (e.g., a card with a 5 is worth 5 points).

However, if you have a sequence of consecutive cards, only the lowest card in the sequence counts towards your score (e.g., having cards 5, 6, and 7 counts only as 5 points).

Each chip counts as -1 point.

Winning

The player with the lowest number of points wins the game. In case of a tie on points, the player with the fewest cards wins.
